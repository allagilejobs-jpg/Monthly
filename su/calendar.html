Tuesday — Tip Post

💡 Tip Template
🕐 12-1 PM

Graphic Text:

Title: Meltdowns ≠ tantrums

Body: Tantrums have a goal. Meltdowns are sensory/emotional overload. The response to each is completely different.

This changed how I parent: 👇 Meltdowns ≠ Tantrums 🔸 TANTRUM: Has a goal (wants something). Child has some control. Will stop if they get what they want. 🔸 MELTDOWN: No goal. Sensory or emotional overload. Child has NO control. Can't just "stop." The response to each is completely different. Understanding this = more patience + better support. 💙 Full guide on our blog (link in bio) #AutismMeltdown #AutismParenting #AutismTips #SensoryOverload #AutismMom
