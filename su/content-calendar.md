## Content Batching Tips

### Weekly Prep (Sunday or Monday)
1. Write 3-5 social captions
2. Create any graphics needed
3. Schedule posts for the week
4. Outline 1 blog post

### Monthly Prep (Last week of month)
1. Plan next month's themes
2. Identify 4 blog topics
3. Create email newsletter outline
4. Review analytics and adjust strategy

## Tools Recommended

**Scheduling:**
- Later or Planoly (Instagram)
- Meta Business Suite (FB + IG)
- Buffer (multiple platforms)

**Graphics:**
- Canva (free tier works great)
- Use consistent brand colors

**Analytics:**
- Instagram Insights
- Google Analytics (for blog)
- Email platform analytics

**Organization:**
- Notion or Trello for content planning
- Google Sheets for tracking
