## 💡 Content Gaps & Fresh Angles

### High-Priority Content Ideas

1. **Misinformation Myth-Busters Series**
   - "3 things TikTok gets wrong about autism"
   - "Signs of autism TikTok says are real (but aren't)"
   - Position as evidence-based, not fear-based

2. **Glass Child Syndrome**
   - Siblings of high-support needs kids who fade into background
   - Underserved topic, high emotional resonance
   - Source: The Autism Cafe covers this

3. **Neurodiversity-Affirming IEP Accommodations**
   - Move beyond traditional asks
   - "No homework accommodation" - parents can request this!
   - Affirming vs. masking-based goals

4. **Autistic Burnout Prevention**
   - Signs of burnout in kids AND adults
   - Started young for many (some report first burnout at age 6)
   - Actionable prevention tips

5. **Autism Acceptance vs. Awareness - The Language Shift**
   - Why the community moved away from "awareness"
   - Acceptance → Inclusion → Appreciation
   - Timely for April content
