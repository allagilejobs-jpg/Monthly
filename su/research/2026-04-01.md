## 🎯 Competitor Content Gaps

### Topics We Should Cover More:
1. **Sibling content** - Most creators focus only on the autistic child
2. **Parent self-discovery** - Few talking about parents realizing their own neurodivergence
3. **Practical IEP templates** - Everyone talks about IEPs, few provide actual templates
4. **Summer planning** - Transition from school to summer (timely for May content)
5. **Teen autism content** - Most focused on young children

### What's Oversaturated:
- Basic "signs of autism" content (unless we add fresh angle)
- Puzzle piece imagery (controversial, avoid)
- Inspiration porn (autistic person doing "normal" thing framed as heroic)
