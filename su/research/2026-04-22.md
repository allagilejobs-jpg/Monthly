# Content Research - April 22, 2026

## 🔥 Trending Topics This Week

### 1. Self-Diagnosis Debate (HOT TOPIC)
The biggest conversation in autism spaces right now is the **self-diagnosis controversy on social media**. Key points:
- MUSC study showing concern about social media-driven self-diagnosis
- Psychology Today article: "Autism in Women: TikTok Diagnosis or Self-Correction?"
- BBC: "False social media posts fuel self-diagnosis" (March 20, 2026)
- Study: ~50% of ADHD/autism content on social media may be misleading
- Counter-argument: Neurodivergent people use social media to find community, not just self-diagnose

**Content Opportunity:** Create balanced content for parents navigating this - "When your teen thinks they're autistic because of TikTok: What to do"

### 2. 2026 Themes
Two major themes circulating:
- **Autism Society of America:** "Celebrate Differences" (#CelebrateDifferences)
- **United Nations (WAAD):** "Autism and Humanity – Every Life Has Value"

### 3. Medical vs Neuroaffirmative Framing
New Frontiers research analyzing how autism is discussed across 5 languages:
- "Medical" framing: disorder, deficit, pathology, treatment, burden
- "Neuroaffirmative" framing: identity, difference, pride, diversity, belonging
- Growing push toward neuroaffirmative language

### 4. Profound Autism Label Debate
AP coverage on the controversial proposal for "profound autism" label for high-support-needs individuals. Could introduce new stigma vs. providing clearer support categories.
