### Post 4: Quick Tip (Carousel idea)
**Caption:**
🚨 Just got an autism diagnosis? Here's what to do THIS WEEK:

1️⃣ Take a breath. Process. It's okay to feel all the feelings.

2️⃣ Call your pediatrician — ask for referrals to:
   • Developmental pediatrician
   • Speech therapy
   • Occupational therapy
   • ABA (if appropriate for your child)

3️⃣ Get on waitlists NOW. Even if you're not sure. Waitlists can be 6-12 months.

4️⃣ Contact your school district about early intervention or IEP evaluation.

5️⃣ Find your people. Join a parent support group (online counts!).

Save this post. Share it with a parent who needs it. 💙

### Post 8: Myth Buster
**Caption:**
MYTH: "Autism is caused by bad parenting"

TRUTH: Absolutely not. Never. Not even a little bit.

Autism is a neurological difference. It's how your child's brain is wired. It's not caused by:
❌ Vaccines
❌ Screen time
❌ Your parenting style
❌ Anything you did or didn't do

If anyone ever implies otherwise, feel free to hand them a science textbook and walk away.

You're a great parent. Your child is lucky to have you. 💙
