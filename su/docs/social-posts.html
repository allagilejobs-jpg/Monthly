Post 5: Myth Buster
MYTH: "Autism is caused by bad parenting" TRUTH: Absolutely not. Never. Not even a little bit. Autism is a neurological difference. It's how your child's brain is wired. It's not caused by: ❌ Vaccines ❌ Screen time ❌ Your parenting style ❌ Anything you did or didn't do If anyone ever implies otherwise, feel free to hand them a science textbook and walk away. You're a great parent. Your child is lucky to have you. 💙 #AutismMyths #AutismFacts #AutismAwareness #AutismAcceptance #AutismParent #AutismEducation #AutismTruths
