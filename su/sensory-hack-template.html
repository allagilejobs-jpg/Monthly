💡 TIP
The "Noise Buffer" Strategy
Keep noise-canceling headphones or earbuds in your bag at ALL times. Even without music, they reduce overwhelming sounds by 20-30dB — perfect for sudden sensory overload moments.
🌟 Pro tip: Practice putting them on at home so it becomes automatic when you need them in public!
