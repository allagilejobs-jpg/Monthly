## 📸 Instagram Caption

🛑 Stop saying "autism awareness."

The autistic community has moved on. Here's why ACCEPTANCE matters more ↓

We've all heard "autism awareness" for years. But here's the thing:

❌ Everyone's already "aware"
❌ It focuses on tragedy, not strengths
❌ It speaks FOR autistic people, not WITH them

✅ ACCEPTANCE embraces autism as valid
✅ It centers autistic voices
✅ It focuses on inclusion, not just information

The progression: Awareness → Acceptance → Inclusion → Appreciation
