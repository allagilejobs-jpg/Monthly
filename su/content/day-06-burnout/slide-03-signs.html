Signs of Burnout

📉 Skills regression (things that were easy become hard)
🔋 Extreme exhaustion that sleep doesn't fix
🚫 Increased sensory sensitivities
😵 Executive function collapse (can't plan, decide, start)
🏠 Withdrawing from everything and everyone
😢 Feeling like you've lost yourself
💥 More frequent meltdowns or shutdowns
