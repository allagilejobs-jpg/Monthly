Recovery &
Prevention Tips
🛑 Reduce demands — drop everything non-essential
🎭 Stop masking where possible — let yourself stim, be quiet, be you
🏠 Create a sensory sanctuary — low lights, quiet, comfort items
⭐ Engage special interests — they're restorative, not frivolous
⏰ Be patient — recovery takes months, not days
🗣️ Seek support — therapy, community, accommodations
