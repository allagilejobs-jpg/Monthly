What Is Autistic Burnout?
A state of chronic exhaustion caused by the cumulative effect of navigating a neurotypical world without adequate support or accommodation.
❌ It's NOT:
• Regular tiredness
• Laziness
• Just depression
• A choice
✅ It IS:
• Neurological exhaustion
• Loss of skills
• Identity crisis
• Recoverable (with support)
