If Considering ABA
Questions to Ask Providers
Vet carefully. Good providers welcome these:
❓ What's your approach to stimming?
❓ How do you handle refusal or "no"?
❓ Who sets the goals — and are they functional?
❓ What does a typical session look like?
❓ How do you measure success?
❓ Can I observe sessions anytime?
❓ What's your view on neurodiversity?
