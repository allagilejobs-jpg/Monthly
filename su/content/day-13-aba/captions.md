## 📸 Instagram Caption

🤔 THE ABA CONTROVERSY: What parents need to know

Is ABA right for your child? Let's look at both sides ⬇️

📋 WHAT IS ABA?
Applied Behavior Analysis — the most researched autism intervention. Covered by most insurance. Practices vary WIDELY.

⚠️ THE CONCERNS (from autistic adults):
• Historical focus on compliance over wellbeing
• Teaching masking, not acceptance
• Suppressing harmless stims
• High intensity (20-40 hrs/week)
• Some report trauma from certain programs

✓ THE OTHER SIDE:
• Modern "naturalistic" ABA looks different
• Some providers are neurodiversity-affirming
• Can help with safety skills & communication
• Often the only insurance-covered option
• Quality varies HUGELY by provider

❓ QUESTIONS TO ASK:
• What's your approach to stimming?
• How do you handle "no"?
• Who sets the goals?
• Can I observe sessions anytime?
• What's your view on neurodiversity?

You know your child best. 💙

This post isn't pro or anti-ABA — it's pro informed decision-making.
