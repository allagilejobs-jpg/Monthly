## 📸 Instagram Caption

🌪️ Supporting your child through MELTDOWNS (during & after)

It's NOT a tantrum. Here's the difference ⬇️

😤 TANTRUM:
• Goal-oriented
• Checks for audience
• Can stop if need is met

🌪️ MELTDOWN:
• Overwhelm response
• Neurological crisis
• Can't just "stop"
• NOT manipulation

❌ DURING — DON'T:
• Yell or raise voice
• Demand eye contact
• Say "calm down"
• Touch without permission
• Take it personally

✅ DURING — DO:
• Stay calm (regulate yourself first)
• Reduce sensory input
• Give space if safe
• Use few, simple words
• Just BE there

💙 AFTER:
• Give recovery time
• Don't lecture or punish
• Reconnect when ready
• Note triggers
• Reassure them they're loved

Meltdowns aren't misbehavior. They're a sign your child needs SUPPORT. 💙
