Meltdown vs Tantrum
😤 TANTRUM
• Goal-oriented
• Wants something specific
• Checks for audience
• Can stop if need is met
• Strategic behavior
🌪️ MELTDOWN
• Overwhelm response
• Neurological crisis
• No audience awareness
• Can't just "stop"
• NOT manipulation
