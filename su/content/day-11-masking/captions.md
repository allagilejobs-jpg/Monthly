🎭 HIGH-MASKING AUTISM: The Hidden Struggle

"They look fine. They're falling apart inside."

What is masking? ⬇️

Masking is when autistic people suppress their natural traits to appear neurotypical. It includes:
• Forcing eye contact
• Mimicking expressions
• Suppressing stims
• Scripting conversations

💔 THE COST:
• Chronic exhaustion
• Higher anxiety & depression
• Loss of identity
• Delayed diagnosis
• Worse mental health outcomes

🚨 SIGNS OF HIGH-MASKING:
• Exhausted after social situations
• Feels like "performing" in public
• Different person at home vs outside
• Scripts conversations in advance
• Studies others to learn "normal"
• Meltdowns in private

You shouldn't have to perform to be accepted.
Unmasking is a journey. You're not alone. 💜
