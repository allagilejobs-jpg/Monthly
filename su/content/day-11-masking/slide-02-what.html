What Is Masking?
Masking (or camouflaging) is when autistic people consciously or unconsciously suppress their natural traits to appear neurotypical and "fit in."
🎭 Masking includes:
• Forcing eye contact
• Mimicking others' expressions
• Suppressing stims
• Scripting conversations
• Hiding sensory discomfort
