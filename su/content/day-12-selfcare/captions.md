## 📸 Instagram Caption

🧘‍♀️ SELF-CARE for autism parents that actually works

Not bubble baths. REAL strategies ⬇️

⏰ MICRO-BREAKS
You don't need an hour. Small moments add up.
• 2 min alone in the bathroom
• 5 min with headphones on
• Deep breaths in the car
• Coffee while it's still HOT

🤝 FIND YOUR PEOPLE
Support groups aren't weakness — they're survival.
• Online autism parent communities
• Local support meetups
• Therapy for caregivers (yes, it's valid!)

🚫 SET BOUNDARIES
"No" is a complete sentence.
• Skip draining events
• Limit unsolicited advice givers
• It's OK to cancel plans

🙋 ASK FOR HELP
This is NOT weakness. It's wisdom.
• Respite care exists for a reason
• Trade childcare with other autism parents
• Be specific about what you need

You can't pour from an empty cup. 💕

What's ONE thing you'll do for yourself today? 👇
