## 📸 Instagram Caption

🪟 Is your neurotypical child becoming invisible?

Let's talk about GLASS CHILD SYNDROME.

When one child has high support needs (autism, disability, chronic illness), their siblings often fade into the background.

"Glass" because parents look right through them to focus on the child who needs more.

These kids become the "perfect child" who never asks for help. But inside, they're struggling.

Swipe to learn the signs and how to help ➡️

⚠️ Warning signs:
• Never asks for help
• Becomes the "perfect child"
• Downplays their own needs
• Shows resentment (then guilt)

💙 How to help:
• Schedule 1-on-1 time
• Celebrate THEIR wins
• Validate ALL their feelings
• Connect them with sibling support
