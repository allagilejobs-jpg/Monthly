🔄 Daily Posting Workflow

Morning (9 AM)

You'll receive an automated reminder with:

  • What's scheduled for today
  • The graphic file path
  • Ready-to-copy caption

Post to Instagram

Step 1: Open the graphic file or use the Editor to customize
Step 2: Copy the caption from the calendar/reminder
Step 3: Post to Instagram with hashtags
Step 4: Share to Stories with engagement sticker (poll, question, etc.)

Best Posting Times

  • Morning: 7-9 AM (parents checking phones)
  • Midday: 12-1 PM (lunch breaks)
  • Evening: 7-9 PM (after kids' bedtime)

🎨 Using the Template Editor

The Template Editor lets you customize any template and export at your desired size.

How to Use

Step 1: Select a template from the dropdown
Step 2: Click any text to edit it directly
Step 3: Choose your export size (Portrait, Square, Story)
Step 4: Click "Download PNG" — image saves automatically
💡 Pro Tip: Use Portrait (1080×1350) for feed posts to maximize screen real estate!

🤖 Automation (Cron Jobs)

Three automated processes help you stay consistent:

📝 Weekly Content Brainstorm

  • When: Sundays @ 8 PM
  • What: Generates 7 fresh post ideas for the week
  • Output: weekly-ideas/YYYY-MM-DD.md
  • Delivery: Summary sent to Telegram

📢 Daily Posting Reminder

  • When: Every day @ 9 AM
  • What: Checks calendar, preps today's content
  • Delivery: Telegram with graphic path + caption

🔍 Content Research

  • When: Wednesdays @ 12 PM
  • What: Trends, awareness dates, fresh angles
  • Output: research/YYYY-MM-DD.md
  • Delivery: Summary sent to Telegram
💡 Note: All automation outputs are saved to files AND pushed to GitHub Pages automatically, so you can always access them here.
