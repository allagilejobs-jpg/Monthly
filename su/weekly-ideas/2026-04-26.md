## 1. 📚 EDUCATIONAL: Interoception - The Hidden 8th Sense
**Post Type:** Carousel (8 slides)
**Best Day:** Monday, April 27
**Best Time:** 9-10 AM

**Hook/Headline:**
"Your child says they're not hungry... then has a meltdown 20 minutes later. It's not defiance. It's interoception."

**Key Points:**
1. Cover: "The Sense No One Talks About" + arrow prompt
2. What is interoception? The ability to feel internal body signals (hunger, thirst, pain, temperature, needing the bathroom)
3. Why it matters for autistic kids: often delayed or muted signals
4. Real examples: not feeling hungry until starving, not noticing pain, toileting difficulties
5. The behavior connection: "behavior problems" are often unrecognized body signals
6. Signs of interoception differences (checklist format)
7. 4 ways to support: body check-ins, visual schedules for eating/drinking, interoception activities, narrate YOUR body signals
8. CTA: "This changed everything for our family. Save + share."

**Hashtags:** #interoception #autismparenting #sensoryprocessing #hiddensense #autismhacks #neurodivergent #spectrumunlocked
