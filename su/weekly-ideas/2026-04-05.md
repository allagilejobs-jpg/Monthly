## 2. 📚 EDUCATIONAL: AAC - Giving Your Child a Voice
**Post Type:** Reel (60 sec) or Carousel  
**Best Day:** Thursday, April 9  
**Best Time:** 9-10 AM ⭐ PEAK

**Hook/Headline:**  
"AAC doesn't replace speech. It builds it. Here's what every parent should know 🗣️"

**Key Points:**
1. AAC = Augmentative & Alternative Communication (tablets, PECS, sign language)
2. MYTH: "AAC will make them lazy and stop trying to talk" - FALSE!
3. TRUTH: Research shows AAC actually INCREASES verbal speech
4. It reduces frustration (communication = connection)
5. Types: low-tech (picture boards), high-tech (tablets/apps)
6. Popular apps: Proloquo2Go, TouchChat, LAMP Words for Life
7. Don't wait for "enough" speech - early AAC helps everyone
8. CTA: "Ask your SLP about AAC today"

**Caption:**
"But if we give them a device, they'll never learn to talk."

This is the #1 myth that keeps kids from communicating.

Here's the truth: AAC (communication devices, apps, picture boards) doesn't REPLACE speech.

It BUILDS it.

Studies show kids who use AAC often INCREASE their verbal words because:
✨ Less frustration = more attempts
✨ They see the power of communication
✨ Language pathways get strengthened

Your child deserves a voice - in whatever form works for THEM.
