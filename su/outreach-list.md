**Engagement Rules:**
1. Join and observe for 1 week before posting
2. Provide value first — answer questions, share tips
3. Don't drop links immediately (against most group rules)
4. Share your site only when directly relevant/asked
5. Build relationships with other engaged parents

## Podcast Pitch List

### Autism-Focused Podcasts
1. **Autism Parenting Magazine Podcast**
   - Pitch: Your story + practical first steps for newly diagnosed families

2. **The Autism Dad Podcast**
   - Pitch: Resources for both autism moms AND dads

3. **All Autism Talk**
   - Pitch: The emotional + practical journey post-diagnosis

4. **The Autism Helper Podcast**
   - Pitch: Parent perspective on early intervention

### Parenting Podcasts (broader reach)
5. **Mom and Dad Are Fighting (Slate)**
   - Pitch: Parenting a child with autism — what I wish I knew

6. **One Bad Mother**
   - Pitch: The diagnosis moment and finding your village

7. **Spawned Parenting Podcast**
   - Pitch: Autism resources that actually help

### Pitch Template:
```
Subject: Podcast Guest Pitch: Autism Parent Resources

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your name], founder of Spectrum Unlocked — a free resource hub for parents navigating an autism diagnosis.

When my child was diagnosed, I frantically Googled at 2 AM and found nothing but overwhelming medical jargon. That experience led me to create the resource I wish existed.

I'd love to share:
- What the first 48 hours post-diagnosis really look like
- The biggest mistakes parents make (and how to avoid them)
- Practical first steps that actually help

My site helps [X] parents each month find clarity during an overwhelming time.

Would this be a good fit for your show?

[Your name]
spectrumunlocked.com
```

## Email Outreach Templates

### To Facebook Group Admins:
```
Subject: Would love to contribute to your community

Hi [Name],

I'm an autism parent and I've been a member of [Group Name] for [time]. The support here has been incredible.

I recently launched Spectrum Unlocked — a free resource hub for parents navigating autism. I've created a "First 48 Hours Post-Diagnosis" guide that I think would really help new members.

Would it be okay to share it with the group? I'm not selling anything — just want to help other parents like me.

Thank you for running such a supportive community.

[Your name]
```

### To Potential Collaborators:
```
Subject: Collaboration idea — helping autism families

Hi [Name],

I love what you're doing with [their work]. Your post about [specific thing] really resonated with me.

I run Spectrum Unlocked — a free resource hub for autism parents. I'm looking to connect with others who serve this community for potential collaboration.

Would you be open to a quick chat? I'd love to explore how we might support each other's audiences.

[Your name]
```
