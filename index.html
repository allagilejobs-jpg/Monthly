Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know

Is my data private? ▼
Your data lives in your browser's local storage by default — it never leaves your device. Cloud sync is optional and uses your personal Firebase account with encrypted transfer.
What file formats are supported? ▼
Bank statements in CSV, PDF, and Excel formats. Grocery data via Excel upload. Receipt photos in JPG, PNG, HEIC, WebP, BMP, and TIFF for OCR scanning.
Can I use this on my phone? ▼
Yes! All dashboards are fully responsive and designed mobile-first. Upload files, scan receipts, and track spending from any device.
How do I share with my partner? ▼
Sign in with the same account on both devices. Cloud sync will keep your data in sync automatically across all logged-in devices.
Is it free? ▼
Yes, completely free. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no ads. Your finances are your business.
